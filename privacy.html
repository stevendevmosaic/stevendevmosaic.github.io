Mosaic Collage Maker

Privacy Policy

Effective 3 July 2026

Overview

Mosaic Collage Maker does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. This app works entirely on your own device and has no network or internet connectivity of any kind.

Your Photos

All photos you load into the app stay on your device. They are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with anyone — including the developer. The app has no cloud storage, no syncing feature, and no way to send your photos anywhere.

No Data Collection

Specifically, this app does not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Use analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind
  • Contain advertising or third-party advertising networks
  • Require an account, sign-in, or registration
  • Share data with any third party, because no data is collected in the first place

Local Storage

The app saves a small number of your own preferences (such as default margins and page size settings) locally on your device, using standard application storage provided by your operating system. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or anyone else.

Children's Privacy

Because this app collects no data of any kind from any user, it does not collect data from children either. The app is not directed at children specifically, but is safe for use by any age given the complete absence of data collection or network activity.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app adds an optional feature that involves data collection — this page will be updated and the effective date above will reflect the change. As of the effective date listed above, this policy accurately describes the app's complete data practices.
