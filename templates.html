Example Templates

Ready-made layouts you can download and fill with your own photos. A template holds the design — frame arrangement, text, pinned branding, page size — but no pictures, so it works with whatever library you point it at.

How to use one

  1. Download a template below — it saves as a small .json file.
  2. In the app, choose Restore and pick that file. The design loads with empty frames.
  3. Load Photos to bring in your own library.
  4. Press 🗂️⚡ From Template to repeat the design across as many pages as you want, with your photos poured into the frames.
Note: a template carries its own page size, so restoring one will set your page setup to match it. Each template's size is listed on its card below. Everything is editable once loaded — treat these as starting points rather than fixed designs.
